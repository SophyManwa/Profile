Real-Time Credit Risk Prediction System

Streaming & ML

A distributed streaming system for scoring credit transactions as they happen rather than after the fact. Event-driven ingestion feeds a PySpark Structured Streaming pipeline, an XGBoost model scores transactions in near real time, and SHAP values are attached to every prediction so a reviewer can see why a score landed where it did. Includes a concept-drift monitor that flags when the model's assumptions start drifting from incoming data.

Apache Kafka · PySpark Streaming · XGBoost · SHAP · Apache Cassandra

Motor Insurance Analytics & Risk Pricing Engine

Data Warehousing

An end-to-end decision-support system for motor insurance pricing: a Python ETL pipeline lands raw policy and claims data into a structured PostgreSQL warehouse, Prefect orchestrates the refresh schedule, and a Scikit-learn model predicts claim severity. A Power BI layer on top gives underwriters a single view of exposure and pricing risk by segment.

Python · PostgreSQL · Scikit-learn · Prefect · SQL · Power BI
